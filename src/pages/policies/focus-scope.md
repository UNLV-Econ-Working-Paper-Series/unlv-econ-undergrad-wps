The **UNLV ECON 495 Journal (Capstone Proceedings)** is an open-access archive of undergraduate working papers produced in **ECON 495 (Seminar in Economic Research)** at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas.

## What we publish

The Proceedings publishes **student-authored working papers** completed as part of ECON 495. Papers typically include an original research question, evidence or data analysis, and a written manuscript suitable for public reading and citation.

## What we do not publish

The Proceedings does **not** publish:

- Peer-reviewed journal articles
- Grades, evaluations, or course materials intended for private instructional use
- Content containing confidential information or restricted data that cannot be shared publicly
- Submissions that do not comply with site policies (including rights, privacy, and integrity requirements)

## Working paper notice (not peer reviewed)

Papers published here are **working papers**. They are **not peer reviewed**, and posting does not imply endorsement, validation, or verification of findings. Authors may revise papers over time.

## Audience

This archive serves students, researchers, alumni, and the public by making undergraduate research **discoverable, readable, and citable** beyond the classroom.
